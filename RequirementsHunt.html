This is something I had going around in my head whilst driving back from Stabcon 2012.

We're Going On A Requirements Hunt!

(With apologies to Michael Rosen)

We're going on a Requirements Hunt.
We're going to catch a full one!
We're not scared.

Oh no! Users! Icky, Picky Users.
We can't go through them.
We can't go around them.
We're going to have to go over them.
Stomp! Stomp! Stomp! Stomp!

We're going on a Requirements Hunt.
We're going to catch a full one!
We're not scared.

Oh no! The Powerpoints! The snoring, boring Powerpoints!
We can't go around them.
We can't go over them.
We're going to have to go through them.
Next! Next! Next! Next!

We're going on a Requirements Hunt.
We're going to catch a full one!
We're not scared.

Oh no! The Accountants! The Cost controlling Accountants!
We can't go through them.
We can't go over them.
We're going to have to go around them.
Tip-toe, tip-toe, tip-toe, tip-toe.

We're going on a Requirements Hunt.
We're going to catch a full one!
We're not scared.

Oh no! A Requirement! A complete and coherent Requirement!
Quick, back around the Accountants!
Tip-toe, tip-toe, tip-toe, tip-toe.
Back through the Powerpoints!
Next! Next! Next! Next!
Back over the Users!
Stomp! Stomp! Stomp! Stomp!
Back to our cubicle.
Slam the door. Oh... no door. We're doomed to failure. Just like normal.
